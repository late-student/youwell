Hi everyone, I am a nurse, and throughout my years of service, I have seen firsthand that true health and wellness require dedication, consistency, and mindful action. From my heart, and inspired by a deep desire to help you care for your well-being, I created these charts to help you actively track and support your health. They are designed to encourage healthy habits, improve your quality of life, and empower you to stay as healthy as possible for as long as possible.

Nowadays, more and more people are living a sedentary life because of work
pressures, inefficient time management, unhealthy diet, etc. There is an increase in
ailments associated with such an unhealthy lifestyle. People are suffering from morbid
obesity, cardiac issues, diabetes, and many other comorbidities that stem from those
ailments. Intake of junk and oily food and not being adequately hydrated is also
deteriorating people’s health. Such issues affect not just the physical state of people but
also the mental state, leading to inability to focus and perform at their optimum levels.
Stress, inadequate relaxation, and no time to nourish their soul have created hostility,
depression and frustration among people that permeate their negative impact constantly
in society. Moreover, stress and mental fatigue can also stem from having inadequate
sleep and inefficient time management and organization, leading to exhaustion and
unbearable frustration throughout the day. I hope to help you attack these problems in a way that helps make noticeable consistency and improvements to your life. 

-Neri
